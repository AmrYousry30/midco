Wellhead Supply

Wellhead Supply MIDCO the sole agent of CST-CSEPEL TECHNO Ltd in Egypt for well head supply.

CST-CSEPEL TECHNO Ltd. is a Hungarian owned private company founded in 1991. Its main profile is the production of complete oil, gas and geothermal production equipment (wellheads, x-mas tree equipment, gate valves, flanged connections, auxiliary drilling parts, etc.) for use under working pressure. CST-CSEPEL TECHNO Ltd. has been producing parts, equipment and flanged connections for the oil industry for more, than 50 years now. The wellheads used at the drilling of wells (with rated working pressures of 3.000, 5.000 and 10.000 psi) have almost all had been produced here according to API requirements.

In 1995 CST-CSEPEL TECHNO Ltd. acquired API 6A certificate and they have been renewing it since then continuously As a major API 6A and API 16A certified manufacturer in Central Europe CST-CSEPEL TECHNO Ltd have international experience gained in four continents.
